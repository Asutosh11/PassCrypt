## Full Description

**Take control of your digital security with PassCrypt—the ultra-secure, offline-first password vault designed for total privacy.**

In an age of constant connectivity and data leaks, PassCrypt stands as your personal digital fortress. We believe your most sensitive information should never touch the cloud. That’s why PassCrypt is built to be **100% offline**, ensuring your passwords stay exactly where they belong: on your device.

### 🛡️ UNCOMPROMISING SECURITY
PassCrypt uses industry-standard **AES-256** encryption to protect your data. Your vault is secured by SQLCipher, ensuring your sensitive credentials are never stored in plain text.
• **Biometric Lock**: Access your vault instantly and securely using your device's fingerprint or face scan.
• **No Internet Required**: PassCrypt does not require internet permission. Your data never leaves your physical device.

### ✨ MINIMALIST & PROFESSIONAL DESIGN
Say goodbye to cluttered interfaces. PassCrypt features a modern, professional minimalist design that prioritizes clarity and ease of use.
• **Adaptive Layout**: Whether you’re on a phone or a tablet (7" or 10"), PassCrypt adapts beautifully. Tablet users enjoy a dedicated Master-Detail layout for professional-grade management.
• **Vivid Blue Aesthetic**: A clean, high-contrast interface that makes navigating your vault a breeze.

### 🚀 POWERFUL FEATURES
• **Instant Search**: Find any credential in seconds with our high-performance search engine.
• **Smart Categorization**: Organise your life into Finance, Work, Social, and more with dedicated icons.
• **Password Generator**: Create strong, unhackable passwords with a single tap.
• **Secure Vault Transfer**: Switch phones easily! Move your data Android-to-Android via point-and-scan encrypted QR Chunks.
• **Cloud-Free Backups**: Export a password-locked PDF for safekeeping or a specialized CSV for direct import into Apple's iCloud Keychain.
• **Zero Tracking**: No ads. No analytics. No background data usage. Just pure security.

### 🔒 WHY CHOOSE PASSCRYPT?
Most password managers store your data on their servers. PassCrypt gives the power back to you. By removing the "Sync" factor, we remove the primary target for hackers. It’s simple, it’s fast, and it’s arguably the most secure way to manage your credentials in 2026.
