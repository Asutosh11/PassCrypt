Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026 (Current Version)

PassCrypt is built as a Privacy-First, Zero-Knowledge offline application. We believe your digital life should belong to you, and you alone.

1. Data Collection & Network

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

  • Zero Network Access: This app does not request the INTERNET permission. It is physically impossible for your data to leave your device.
  • No Servers: We do not maintain any back-end servers or cloud storage.
  • No Analytics: We do not track usage, collect telemetry, or use any tracking SDKs.

2. Data Storage & Encryption

All credentials are stored exclusively on your device in a secure vault.

  • Military-Grade Encryption: Data is scrambled using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • The Master Key: Your encryption key (Master Key) is tied strictly to your device's secure credentials (Biometrics or PIN/Password). This key never leaves the secure enclave of your hardware hardware (Trusted Execution Environment).

3. Third-Party Access

Because we have no servers and no network access, no third party can access your data through us. We do not sell, trade, or share your information. Our business model is based on your trust, not your data.

4. Hardware Permissions

  • Biometrics/PIN: Used only for local vault authentication. We never see or store your biometric data (fingerprint/face) or your device passcode.
  • Storage: Used solely to maintain the encrypted local database file on your device's persistent storage.

5. Your Rights & Data Control

You have absolute control. Your data is yours. Deleting the application or clearing application data via system settings will permanently erase all credentials, as we hold no backups and have no way to recover your data.
